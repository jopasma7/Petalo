## Why Pétalo?

Running a flower shop means juggling stock that expires, orders tied to specific events, dozens of client relationships, and suppliers you depend on every week. Most florists manage this with notebooks, spreadsheets, or nothing at all — which works until it doesn't.

**Pétalo replaces all of that** with a purpose-built desktop app that's fast, offline, and simple enough to use without training. There's no cloud to connect to, no monthly fee to a platform you don't control, and no learning curve that takes time away from your actual business.

## Features

### Dashboard
- Real-time KPIs: today's orders, revenue this month, products with low stock
- Upcoming events panel — never miss a delivery date
- Quick-access navigation to any section

### Products & Categories
- Full product catalog with categories, stock control, and cost/price tracking
- Low-stock alerts with configurable thresholds
- Bulk export to PDF
- Supplier linking per product

### Clients
- Complete client registry: name, phone, email, address, type (Regular, VIP, Corporate...)
- Client history and purchase tracking
- Advanced search and filtering

### Events & Orders
- Link orders to specific events: weddings, birthdays, baptisms, funerals, corporate
- Order status tracking: Pending → In Progress → Ready → Delivered
- PDF generation per order
- Special notes and delivery date management

### Inventory & Suppliers
- Inventory movement log with full history
- Purchase orders: automatic suggestions and manual creation
- Supplier management with contact details and linked products
- Stock alerts: low stock, critical stock, out of stock

### Bilingual Interface
- Full English and Spanish support
- Language selected during onboarding, changeable anytime from settings
- All PDF reports adapt to your chosen language

### PDF Reports
- Generate professional reports from any section
- Reports include your company name, address, and contact details
- Print-ready layout

### Settings & Customization
- Company info used across all reports
- Language, currency (EUR / USD / GBP / MXN), and date format
- Data stored locally — you own your data
